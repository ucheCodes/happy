About Chef Favour

Chef Favour is a seasoned cook. A graduate of Hotel Management and Catering from the renowned Shekinah school of catering, hotel Management and Fashion Design, Immaculate Heart Avenue, Aba, Abia State, Nigeria. Chef Favour has over 5 years experience in all forms of cooking and catering services including cooking of continental and local dishes, baking, decorations etc. She has worked a few years in Silver's Catering Event Management And Home Kitchen. Chef Favour currently delved into the amazing world of computer technology and hopes to be a seasoned computer programmer. She's fascinated with the beauty and awesomeness of graphical user interface design and hopes to be a professional front end, user experience and user interface designer.
